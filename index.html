Interpolating Strange Attractors

The term "strange attractor" is sometimes used in mathematics to describe a chaotic system that seems to be attracted to an ideal behavior. In this project, I was interested in what would happen if I combined multiple chaotic particle systems together. In the demo, you can give a "weight" to each system by increasing or decreasing its slider. Check out the live demo and see what interesting patterns you can create. Use WASD keys or drag/touch to rotate. Scroll mousewheel to zoom in and out.
